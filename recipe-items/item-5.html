Basic Pizza Dough - Makes 1

Ingredients

• 1 teaspoon dried yeast
• ¼ teaspoon caster (superfine) sugar
• ¾ cup (185ml) lukewarm water
• 1½ cups (225g) plain (all-purpose) flour
• ½ teaspoon fine salt

Method

1. Combine the yeast, sugar and water in a bowl.
2. Set aside in a warm place for 10 minutes or until bubbles appear.
3. Place the flour and salt in a bowl and make a well in the centre.
4. Add the yeast mixture and stir until it comes together.
5. Cover with a clean tea towel and set aside in a warm place for 20–30 minutes or until the dough has doubled in size.
