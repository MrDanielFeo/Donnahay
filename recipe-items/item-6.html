Vegie Burger - Serves 4

Ingredients

• 150g firm silken tofu
• 1 x 420g can soy beans, drained
• 1 cup (240g) grated carrot
• 2 tablespoons crunchy peanut butter
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 4 hamburger buns, halved and toasted
• 4 lettuce leaves
• ¾ cup grated fresh beetroot
• 4 slices tomato
• 2 tablespoons yoghurt
• 2 tablespoons tahini
• 1 tablespoon sweet chilli sauce
• 2 tablespoons water

Method

1. Place the tofu between sheets of absorbent paper and press to remove excess water.
2. Place soy beans, carrot, parsley, peanut butter, cumin and tofu into a food processor and process until finely chopped.
3. Shape into 4 patties and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

To make the tahini dressing, combine the yoghurt, tahini, chilli sauce and water and set aside.

1. Heat the oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
2. Add the patties and cook for 4 minutes each side or until golden.
3. Top bun bases with lettuce, beetroot, a patty, tomato and sandwich with bun tops.
4. Serve with the tahini dressing.
