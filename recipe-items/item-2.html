Jam Drop Cookies - Makes 24

Ingredients

• 2 cups (300g) buckwheat flour
• 1½ cups (180g) almond meal (ground almonds)
• 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
• ½ teaspoon sea salt flakes
• ¾ cup (180ml) maple syrup
• ½ cup (125ml) vegetable oil
• ⅓ cup (110g) cherry jam

Method

1. Preheat oven to 160°C (325°F).
2. Place the flour, almond meal, cinnamon, salt, maple syrup and oil in a bowl and mix to combine.
3. Roll heaped tablespoonfuls of the mixture into balls and place on baking trays lined with non-stick baking paper, allowing a little room to spread.
4. Press your thumb gently into the middle of each cookie and fill with ½ teaspoon of the jam.
5. Bake for 14–16 minutes or until golden and crisp.
